How to play

  1. Choose a board to play on: Triangle, English or European.
  2. To start, select a "●" that is two spaces away from the starting "○." Then, select the "○."
  3. The jumped "●" will automati- cally be removed, leaving a "○" in its place.
  4. Jumps may be made horizontally or vertically (or, in the case if the Triangle board, at 45° angles).
  5. The objective of the game is to remove all of the "●," leaving only one.

Results

  1. Leave only one: you're a genius.
  2. Leave two: you're "purty" smart.
  3. Leave three: you're just plain dumb.
  4. Leave four or more: you're an "eg-no-ra-moose."
